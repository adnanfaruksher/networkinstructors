// learning paths

Find Your Track

Structured paths for every level, from first-time networkers to engineers adding firewall or automation skills.

Network Associate

CCNA

Start here if you're new to networking. Build the foundation that every network job demands.

  • Network fundamentals & OSI model
  • Ethernet, VLANs & STP
  • IPv4/IPv6 routing & OSPF
  • ACLs, NAT & DHCP
  • Wireless & WAN connectivity
  • CCNA 200-301 exam prep

Network Professional

CCNP

For engineers ready to move beyond associate level into enterprise architecture and advanced routing.

  • Advanced OSPF, EIGRP & BGP
  • Layer 3 redundancy & QoS
  • SD-WAN & Cisco Viptela
  • Infrastructure automation
  • ENCOR 350-401 + ENARSI

Firewall Specialist

FIREWALL

Deep-dive into next-gen firewall platforms. Policy design, VPN, threat inspection, and HA.

  • FortiGate platform deep-dive
  • Security policy & profiles
  • IPSec & SSL VPN tunnels
  • NAT models & HA modes
  • Troubleshooting & packet flow
  • Fortinet FCP / NSE 4 prep

Automation Engineer

PYTHON

Turn CLI knowledge into code. Automate multi-vendor environments with Python and Ansible.

  • Python for network engineers
  • Netmiko & NAPALM
  • Ansible playbooks & inventory
  • REST APIs & FortiOS automation
  • Git & CI/CD for network config
